OUR STORY

Founded in 2015, Nebula Brew began as a dream to create a coffee experience that transcends the ordinary. Our founder, a former astrophysicist turned coffee roaster, combined scientific precision with artistic passion to develop our unique blends.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Indonesia, ensuring fair trade practices and the highest quality. Each batch is small-roasted in-house to bring out the distinctive characteristics of the bean.

At Nebula Brew, we believe coffee should be an adventure. That's why we're constantly experimenting with new flavors, techniques, and presentations to take your coffee experience to new heights.
